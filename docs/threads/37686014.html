“You're very welcome.” He nods, then stalks toward the opposite cave entrance. “Bye, now.”

You follow his instructions, and make your way towards Averron City proper. Footsteps echo through the tunnels around you; you maneuver carefully through caves lit by luminescent stone outcroppings. Nearby, a waterfall pours from a small rift in the ceiling, rolling gently off rocks and running through a gap into the foggy abyss below. Faintly glowing mushrooms grow in earthen patches in the corners of covered tunnels and caves. It grows cold. You feel uneasy, like something could be watching you from the darkness, and decide to pick up your pace.
